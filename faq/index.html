What is K1 fishing?

K1 fishing usually refers to arcade-style gameplay where users check game rules, balance type, session history, and whether screenshots show demo or real balance.

Can a screenshot prove real earnings?

A screenshot alone is not enough. Check wallet history, cash history, withdrawal proof, account time, and whether the same image appears in promotional posts.

What is the difference between demo balance and real balance?

Demo balance is used for practice or examples. Real balance should connect to wallet history and transaction proof.

Which K1 games should beginners check first?

Beginners should read risk levels, start with small-balance explanations, and avoid judging a game only by win screenshots or social claims.

Why does game history not match wallet history?

Game history and wallet history can track different events. Compare timestamps, balance changes, and transaction status before making a support claim.
