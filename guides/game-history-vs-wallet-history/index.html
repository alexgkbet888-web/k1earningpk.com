Why are these two histories different?

Game history shows what happened inside a round or session. Wallet history shows whether value actually moved into a cash-visible account area. A screenshot of one without the other can be misleading, especially when demo, bonus, or promotional balances are also present.

Evidence to keep before the next step

Check whether the screenshot shows a round result only, a wallet entry only, or both. Save the balance label, time, and any demo or bonus tag. If the site uses separate ledgers for game outcomes and withdrawable balance, a single cropped image is rarely enough to prove a real earning result.

FAQ

Is game history enough to prove K1 Game real earning?

Not by itself. It should be checked with wallet history and visible balance labels.

Why do some screenshots look profitable but still not prove cash?

They may show only round results, demo play, or bonus balance instead of wallet movement.

What is the best evidence mix?

A game result, matching wallet entry, visible balance label, and time match together form a stronger proof set.
