Why do H5 earning screenshots confuse users?

H5 pages often show lightweight result screens that do not clearly separate real balance, bonus credit, demo mode, or game-only outcomes. A single screenshot can look profitable without proving wallet movement.

Evidence to keep before the next step

Keep the H5 result screen, wallet or balance screen, time stamp, and any label showing demo, bonus, cash, or promotional status. Without the label context, the image is weak evidence.
