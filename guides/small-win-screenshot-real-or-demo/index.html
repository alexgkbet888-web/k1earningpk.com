Quick Answer

A small win screenshot is not enough by itself. Check whether the image shows real balance, demo balance, bonus balance, or just a round result.

Action Checklist

1. What a useful screenshot should include

  • The balance label near the result.
  • The game screen or round context.
  • A nearby wallet or history screen if value changed.

2. Why demo and bonus balances confuse people

  • A round result can look impressive even in a demo environment.
  • Bonus balance may not be directly withdrawable.
  • A cropped image often removes the exact labels users need.

3. Move from screenshot to stronger proof

  • Pair the image with wallet or transaction history.
  • Keep device time visible when possible.
  • If the screenshot is too limited, route to the earning-proof checklist.

Evidence to Save

  • Game result screenshot with balance label
  • Wallet or history page
  • Bonus / demo / cash label
